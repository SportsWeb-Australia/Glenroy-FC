Our Story

Since 1946

Founded in 1946, the Glenroy Football Club — proudly known as the Wallabies — has been a cornerstone of footy in Melbourne's north for more than 75 years. Playing in red and white out of Sewell Reserve, the club has enjoyed success across every grade of the Essendon District Football League, including A Grade premierships in 1966, 1982, 1983 and 1986. From Auskick to seniors, Glenroy is a family club built on community, mateship and a love of the game.
